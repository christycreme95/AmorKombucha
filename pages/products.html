Los Sabores

MEXICAN-American KOMBUCHA
Our Kombucha is organic and handmade with love - it is not pasteurized, nor does it contain preservatives. We use local flavors inspired by Mexico. It is made in small batches using traditional techniques. If made the real way, it can take up to 4 weeks to make a bottle. That’s how Amor makes it every day.

A Sip For Justice
The modern system of agriculture often exploits farmers, the environment and our health. We work to obtain our ingredients from organic local farms that employ ethical business practices.

From Farm to Bottle
We obtain the highest quality organic ingredients from San Joaquin County. Greatness grows here and with Amor we want to showcase what the heart of the central valley offers. We love taking advantage of our rich agriculture and diverse ingredients to make the highest quality brew. Local organic agriculture is the best way to support our environment and local communities.
* Black tea is the only ingredient that we can not obtain locally.

Piñata

Jasmine green tea base, pineapple, ginger and turmeric.

Piñata has a jasmine green tea base and pineapple bursting with flavor and the perfect blend of ginger and turmeric to mellow out the experience.

Salud

Tamarind & Chaga.

Tamarindo is a sweet and citrusy flavor and it’s balanced out with Chaga. Chaga is a mushroom that has adaptogenic properties and can help your body adapt to stress and calm down. Together these ingredients give you a familiar taste of the Latin made tamarind drinks so refreshing and packed with flavor.

Eterno

Organic, Fair Trade Jasmine Green tea.

Eterno is refreshing, crisp and abundant in floral aroma that leaves you feeling ready to face the world with amór.

Como La Flor

Organic Hibiscus, Lavender & Rose.

Como la Flor is a caffeine free, refreshing kombucha. Smooth hibiscus and light notes of lavender and rose. This is a bouquet in a bottle.
