Meet Amór Kombucha

Meet our Stockton, CA Native Founders, Alejandro Espino and Martín Sanchez aka the “Kombuchadores or Kombucheros.” We can’t wait to share this premium quality, locally brewed elixir with our Mexican American essence! We are here to build culture, awareness around fermented food and support our great community. Community is the core of Amór Kombucha and we are humbled to be able to serve the people in our great community.

Kombuchamor (con mucho amór), We look forward to meeting each one of you and sharing our beautifully crafted bebidas (drinks) and promise to always, Lead With Love!

What Is Amór Kombucha?

Kombucha is a fermented tea that naturally contains probiotics, organic acids and healthy nutrients. The history of this elixir goes back thousands of years to ancient China. We believe that kombucha is a perfect blend between science and art amór. Kombucha is an expression of our creativity, innovation and functionality. We invest in proper testing to make sure you are drinking the best quality kombucha while never sacrificing our integrity in the process. Our kombucha is the fuel you need to face the world with amór.

We are served on tap at Cast Iron Trading Company in Stockton! Also, find out how to order delivery on our products page!
