Meet Amor Kombucha

Meet our Stockton, CA Native Founders, Alejandro Espino and Martín Sanchez aka the “Kombuchadores or Kombucheros” We can’t wait to share this premium quality, locally brewed elixir with our Mexican American essence!

Our Kombucha is organic and handmade with love, it is not pasteurized, nor does it contain preservatives. We use local flavors inspired by Mexico. It is made in small batches using traditional techniques. We are here to build culture, awareness around fermented food and support our great community.

From Farm To Bottle

We work to obtain our ingredients from organic local farms that employ ethical business practices. We obtain the highest quality organic ingredients from San Joaquin County. Greatness grows here and with Amor we want to showcase what the heart of the central valley offers. We love taking advantage of our rich agriculture and diverse ingredients to make the highest quality brew. Local organic agriculture is the best way to support our environment and local communities.

*Black tea is the only ingredient that we can not obtain locally.

What Is Amor Kombucha?

Kombucha is a fermented tea that naturally contains probiotics, organic acids, enzymes and healthy nutrients. Our kombucha is handmade with AMÓR 100% authentic. Kombucha is traditionally made with tea, water, organic cane sugar(for the culture, not for us to consume), and the SCOBY (symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast). We stick to the script but add a Latin inspired touch since we are a Mexican-American owned company. We brew in small batches to ensure quality and integrity in our kombucha.
